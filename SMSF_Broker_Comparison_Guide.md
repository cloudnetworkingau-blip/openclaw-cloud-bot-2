## ⚠️ Important SMSF Brokerage Rules

### Compliance Must-Haves:
1. **Separate Account:** SMSF must have its own brokerage account
2. **Trustee Authority:** Only trustees can trade
3. **Record Keeping:** All statements must be kept 10+ years
4. **No Mixed Assets:** Cannot combine personal and SMSF holdings

### Tax Considerations:
1. **CGT Records:** Brokerage statements are primary evidence
2. **Dividend Statements:** Not applicable for PMGOLD
3. **Annual Summary:** Needed for SMSF return

### Audit Requirements:
1. **Annual Statements:** Must be provided to auditor
2. **Trade Confirmations:** Keep all records
3. **CHESS Statements:** Important for ownership verification

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

A: NO. SMSF must have separate account.

**Q: How long does account setup take?**
A: Typically 2-5 business days with document verification.

**Q: What if my SMSF already has a brokerage account?**
A: Use existing account if it supports ETF trading.

**Q: Can I set up automatic investments in PMGOLD?**
