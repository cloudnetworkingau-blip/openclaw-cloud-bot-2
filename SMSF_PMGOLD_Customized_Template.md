**Identified Risks:**
1. **Concentration Risk:** 96.2% single asset → EXTREME
2. **Liquidity Risk:** Property illiquid, only $30,000 cash → HIGH
3. **Market Risk:** 100% exposure to Australian property → HIGH
4. **Tenant Risk:** Single tenant dependency → MEDIUM

### 2. Why Gold Addresses These Risks

**Diversification Benefit:**
- Gold has low/negative correlation with property
- Historical performance during property downturns
- Different drivers (monetary vs real estate factors)

**Liquidity Enhancement:**
- PMGOLD: Sell anytime ASX open (2-day settlement)
- Property: 60-120+ days to sell
- Emergency access without selling property

**Inflation Protection:**
- Property: Real asset, provides some inflation protection
- Gold: Monetary asset, different inflation hedge
- Dual protection strategy

### 3. Why PMGOLD Specifically for SMSF

**Compliance Advantages:**
1. **Not collectable:** Avoids strict storage rules
2. **No storage issues:** Perth Mint handles storage
3. **Audit friendly:** Easy verification via brokerage statements
4. **Cost effective:** 0.15% fee vs physical storage (0.5-1%+)

**Investment Advantages:**
1. **Government backing:** Perth Mint + WA Government guarantee
2. **Lowest fee:** 0.15% vs 0.25-0.59% for competitors
3. **Redeemable:** Can convert to physical gold at Perth Mint
4. **Liquid:** Trade like any ASX stock
