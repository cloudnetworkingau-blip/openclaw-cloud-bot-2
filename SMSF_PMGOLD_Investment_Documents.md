**From:** [TRUSTEE NAME]

**Subject:** Investment Rationale for AUD $30,000 PMGOLD Allocation

### 1. Portfolio Context

**Current SMSF Portfolio Characteristics:**
- Primary exposure: Australian real estate
- Limited defensive assets
- Concentration risk in single asset class (property)

**Problem Statement:** The SMSF portfolio lacks adequate diversification and defensive positioning against potential property market corrections, inflation, and economic uncertainty.

### 2. Why Gold?

**Historical Role of Gold:**
- Store of value for millennia
- Hedge against inflation and currency devaluation
- Performs during geopolitical uncertainty
- Low/negative correlation with equities and property

**2025-2026 Market Context:**
- Gold returned +52.6% in AUD terms in 2025
- Currently 18% below January 2026 highs
- Bullish outlook from major institutions (JPMorgan: US$5,000-6,000/oz)
- Central bank accumulation continues

### 3. Why PMGOLD Specifically?

**Advantages over Physical Gold for SMSF:**
1. **Compliance Simplicity:** Not classified as collectable
2. **No Storage Issues:** Perth Mint handles storage
3. **Audit Friendly:** Easy verification via brokerage statements
4. **Cost Effective:** 0.15% fee vs physical storage costs (0.5-1%+)
5. **Liquidity:** Trade like any ASX stock

**Advantages over Other Gold ETFs:**
1. **Government Backing:** Perth Mint + WA Government guarantee
2. **Lowest Fee:** 0.15% vs 0.25-0.59% for competitors
3. **Redeemable:** Can convert to physical gold if desired
4. **Australian:** No currency hedging complexities
