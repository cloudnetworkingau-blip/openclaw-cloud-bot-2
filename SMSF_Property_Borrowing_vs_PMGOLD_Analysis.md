## 🏠 OPTION 1: Borrow for Additional Property (LRBA Strategy)

### **How LRBA Works:**
1. **Limited Recourse Borrowing Arrangement** required
2. **Property held in bare trust** (like your current property)
3. **Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR):** Typically 60-80%
4. **Interest Rates:** 5-7% (higher than standard loans)
5. **Deposit Required:** 20-40% + costs

### **Tax Benefits of Additional Property:**

#### **1. Immediate Tax Deductions:**
- **Interest payments:** Deductible at 15% SMSF tax rate
- **Property expenses:** Rates, insurance, maintenance (deductible)
- **Depreciation:** Building and fixtures (deductible)

#### **2. Long-term Tax Benefits:**
- **Capital gains:** Taxed at 15% in accumulation (10% after 12 months)
- **Pension phase:** Potentially 0% tax on rental income and capital gains
- **Negative gearing:** Losses offset other SMSF income

#### **3. Leverage Benefits:**
- **Amplified returns:** Use borrowed money to buy larger asset
- **Inflation hedge:** Property historically keeps pace with inflation
- **Rental income:** Taxed at concessional 15% rate

### **Example Scenario: Borrow $500,000**

**Assumptions:**
- Property price: $625,000
- Deposit: $125,000 (20%)
- Loan: $500,000 (80% LVR)
- Interest rate: 6.5%
- Rental yield: 4% ($25,000 annually)

**Annual Cash Flow:**
- Rental income: $25,000
- Interest: $32,500 (6.5% of $500k)
- Other expenses: $5,000
- **Net loss:** $12,500

**Tax Benefit:**
- Loss of $12,500 × 15% tax rate = **$1,875 tax saving annually**

**Equity Build:**
- Principal repayment: ~$15,000 annually
- Capital growth (assume 3%): $18,750 annually
- **Total equity increase:** ~$33,750 annually

## 🥇 OPTION 2: PMGOLD Safety Net Strategy

### **Tax Benefits of PMGOLD:**

#### **1. Simplicity & Certainty:**
- **No borrowing costs:** 0.15% management fee only
- **No interest payments:** No cash flow pressure
- **No property expenses:** No maintenance, rates, insurance

#### **2. Defensive Tax Benefits:**
- **Capital gains:** 15% tax (10% after 12 months)
- **No income tax:** PMGOLD doesn't pay dividends
- **Loss offset:** If gold declines, capital loss offsets other gains

#### **3. Risk Management Benefits:**
- **Portfolio insurance:** Protects against property downturn
- **Liquidity:** Sell anytime vs property (60-120+ days)
- **Diversification:** Reduces extreme concentration risk

### **Example Scenario: $30,000 PMGOLD + Build to 10%**

**Phase 1 (Now):** $30,000 PMGOLD
**Phase 2 (2 years):** Build to $78,000 (10% of portfolio)

**Annual Costs:**
- Management fee: 0.15% = $117 on $78,000
- **No interest, no property expenses**

**Tax Position:**
- No ongoing tax deductions
- Capital gains taxed when sold (15%/10%)
- **Simplified compliance**
